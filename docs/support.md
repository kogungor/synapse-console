# Support

## Before Opening an Issue

Please check the [FAQ](../FAQ.md) — your question may already be answered there.

Search [existing issues](https://github.com/kogungor/synapse-console/issues) to see if the bug or request has already been reported.

---

## Bug Reports

Open a [bug report](https://github.com/kogungor/synapse-console/issues/new?template=bug_report.md).

Please include:
- iOS version and device model
- Synapse Console version (Settings → About → Version)
- Steps to reproduce
- What you expected vs. what happened

The more detail you provide, the faster we can investigate.

---

## Feature Requests

Open a [feature request](https://github.com/kogungor/synapse-console/issues/new?template=feature_request.md).

Before requesting, check the [Roadmap](../ROADMAP.md) — it may already be planned.

---

## Billing & Subscription Issues

Billing is handled entirely by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information.

For refunds, billing disputes, or subscription management:
- [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com)
- App Store → your profile → Subscriptions → Synapse Console

**Do not open GitHub issues for billing questions** — we cannot process refunds or access subscription details.

---

## Response Time

This is an indie app maintained by a small team. We monitor issues regularly but cannot guarantee a specific response time. Issues with clear reproduction steps are prioritized.
