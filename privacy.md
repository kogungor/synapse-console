
#Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 24, 2025
1. Introduction

Welcome to PhiloEs ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and disclosed by PhiloEs.
2. Information We Collect

PhiloEs is designed to be privacy-friendly. We do not require you to create an account to use the application.

    Usage Data: We do not collect personal usage data. All progress and settings are stored locally on your device using your browser's Local Storage.
    Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, or phone numbers unless you voluntarily provide them when contacting support.

3. Local Storage

We use local storage to improve your experience, such as caching verb data to make the app load faster and work offline. This data stays on your device and is not sent to our servers.
4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for hosting and analytics that collect anonymous data to help us understand how the app is used and improve performance.
